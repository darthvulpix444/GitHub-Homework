#1 — There is nothing outside of yourself that can ever enable you to get better, stronger, richer, quicker, or smarter. Everything is within. Everything exists. Seek nothing outside of yourself.

#2 — You must understand that there is more than one path to the top of the mountain

#3 — Truth is not what you want it to be; it is what it is, and you must bend to its power or live a lie.

#4 — The purpose of today’s training is to defeat yesterday’s understanding.

#5 — Anger. Control your anger. If you hold anger toward others, they have control over you. Your opponent can dominate and defeat you if you allow him to get you irritated.

#6 — No Fear, No Hesitation, No Surprise, No Doubt

#7— Determine that today you will overcome your self of the day before, tomorrow you will win over those of lesser skill, and later you will win over those of greater skill

# 8 — In time, all things work to your advantage when you pursue them with an open heart.

#9 — All man are the same except for their belief in their own selves, regardless of what others may think of them

#10 — Step by step walk the thousand-mile road.
